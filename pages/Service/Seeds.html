Seeds

Seeds are the reproductive units of flowering plants (angiosperms) and some gymnosperms. They are the result of the fertilization process and contain the embryo, which has the potential to grow into a new plant.

Planting Seeds

Planting seeds is a rewarding and essential activity for gardeners and farmers. By understanding the specific needs of different seeds and providing the right conditions for germination and growth, you can successfully cultivate a variety of plants. Proper preparation, care, and attention to detail are key to ensuring healthy seedlings and a bountiful harvest.
