Garden care is all about keeping your outdoor space flourishing. It involves a variety of tasks to maintain the health and beauty of your plants, lawn, and overall landscaping. Here's a breakdown of some common garden care practices.

Lawn Garden Care

Mowing: Maintain a regular mowing schedule throughout the growing season. In hot and dry climate, this might mean less frequent mowing (every 2-3 weeks) compared to temperate regions. Aim for a mowing height of 2-3 inches to keep the grass healthy and discourage weeds.
